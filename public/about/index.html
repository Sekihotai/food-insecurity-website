Food insecurity—the condition assessed in the food security survey and represented in USDA food security reports—is a household-level economic and social condition of limited or uncertain access to adequate food. Hunger is an individual-level physiological condition that may result from food insecurity.

We are PLICA (aka "The Philadelphia Local Initiative for Community Assistance"). We are a group dedicated to assisting against food insecurity. On this website, you will be shown various sites and locations to either guide you to places to get food or information on how to volunteer. The website includes sites such as volunteering options for nearby food banks, web apps to help people apply for gov help, and directing those suffering toward the nearest bank/pantry. We believe with this website we make it more convenient for users to access this type of information with a few clicks and reduce or possibly stop hunger.
